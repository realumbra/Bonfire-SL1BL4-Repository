List of SL1 and BL4 Legends

As stated on the main page, Legends are those who complete their SL1/BL4 runs with added disadvantages, such as no roll/block/parry, +0 weapons or NG+5.

In this secondary page, you can check every Legend and their runs. Some of them also include content past the specific run that awarded them the title, and might be good examples if you want to learn and improve on your own skills.
